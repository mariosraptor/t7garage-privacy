Privacy Policy for Photo Metering

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Photo Metering is a free photography calculator app for Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Data We Collect

Photo Metering does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline and does not require an account or login.

Advertising

Photo Metering uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve ads, including device information and advertising identifiers. For more information, see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google AdMob. We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any other third-party SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Photo Metering does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted at this URL.
