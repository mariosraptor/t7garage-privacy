# Privacy Policy for T7 Garage

Last updated: March 2026

## Introduction
T7 Garage ("the App") is a free mobile application for Yamaha Tenere 700 riders. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Store
The App stores your "Done Mods" list locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics
- We do not show advertisements
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third Party Links
The App contains links to third party websites (product retailers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites.
