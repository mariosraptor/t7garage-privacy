T7 Garage – Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026

T7 Garage is a free app built for Yamaha Ténéré 700 riders. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

Account & Community Features
When you create an account to use community features, we collect:
- Email address and username (required for registration)
- Profile information you choose to provide: bio, country, km ridden, bike year, profile photo
- Content you post: build photos, captions, mod lists
- Social interactions: likes, comments, follows

Push Notifications
If you grant permission, we collect your device push token to send you notifications when:
- Someone likes your build
- Someone comments on your build
- Someone you follow posts a new build
You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Service Log
Your service log entries are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload this data.

Mod Tracker
Your mod selections are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload this data.

2. ADVERTISING (Google AdMob)
T7 Garage displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized ads including device identifiers, IP address and app usage data.
You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY
Community data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use Row Level Security to ensure users can only access and modify their own data.

4. DATA SHARING
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- Supabase: database and storage hosting
- Google AdMob: advertising
- Expo: push notifications

5. YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time
- Opt out of personalized advertising

To request account deletion contact: t7garageapp@gmail.com

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
T7 Garage is not directed at children under 13.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
